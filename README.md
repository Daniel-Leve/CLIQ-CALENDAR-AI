CalendarAI – Zoho Cliq + Google Calendar + AI

CalendarAI is a smart assistant inside Zoho Cliq that talks to Google Calendar and helps you schedule using plain English.

What it can do

Natural language scheduling  
  Type things like: `Schedule meeting tomorrow at 3 PM for 1 hour`.

Understands your intent (Perplexity AI)
  Pulls out title, date, time, duration, and priority from free‑form text.

Checks your calendar first
  Looks for conflicts before creating events and shows busy time slots.

Handy slash commands
  - `/schedule [task details]`
  - `/today` – today’s events  
  - `/week` – this week’s events  
  - `/update [event] change time to [new time]`  
  - `/delete [event] on [date]`  
  - `/balance` – work‑life balance view  
  - `/suggestplan` – AI suggests an optimized plan for your day

Today’s Tasks widget
  Shows a live summary of today’s meetings and tasks right in Cliq.
